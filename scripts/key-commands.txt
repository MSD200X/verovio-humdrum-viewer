<p><b> Navigation commands <b>

?	toggle this help menu	.
&larr;/&rarr;	next/previous page	.
<i>home</i>	go to first page	.
<i>end</i>	go to last page	.
<i>space</i>	start/stop score playback	.
,	toggle CSV/TSV display of Humdrum data	.
< >	+/&ndash; tab size in editor	.
<nobr>shft-&larr;/&rarr;</nobr>	next/previous work	.
shft-&uarr;	go back to repertory index	.
esc	unfocus graphic element(s)	.

<b> More commands (+<i>alt</i> key) <b>
b	show/hide logo	.
f	(un)freeze dynamic notation	/commands/alt-f
g	display SVG text for current page	.
h	show Humdrum data	.
l	toggle layer coloring	/commands/alt-l
m	show MEI data	/commands/alt-m
o	toggle original clefs	/commands/alt-o
p	show source scan (if available)	.
r	reload file from server	.
s	save to local file	.
v	toggle vi/plain editor	/commands/alt-v
y	show/hide data editor	.

<p><b> Note editing commands </b></p>

a	force stem up	.
b	force stem down	.
c	clear stem direction	.
i	toggle editorial accidental	.
m/M	toggle lower mordent	.
n	toggle natural on note	.
p	toggle pedal start before	.
P	toggle pedal stop after	.
q	toggle grace-note style	.
s	add slur to next note	/graphic/slurs
t/T	toggle trill	.
w/W	toggle upper mordent	.
x	force display of accidental	/graphic/pitch
#	toggle sharp on note	/graphic/pitch
'	toggle staccato	.
-	toggle flat on note	/graphic/pitch
;	toggle fermata	.
^	toggle accent	.
shft-^	toggle marcato	.
`	toggle staccatissimo	.
~	toggle tenuto	.
@	toggle mark	/graphic/marks
&uarr;/&darr;	up/down step	/graphic/pitch
# &uarr;/&darr;	up/down interval	/graphic/pitch
shft-&uarr;/&darr;	up/down octave	/graphic/pitch

&larr;	move slur start 1 note left	.
&rarr;	slur start 1 note right	.
#&larr;	slur start # notes left	.
#&rarr;	slur start # notes right	.

<nobr># shft-&larr;</nobr>	slur end # notes left	.
<nobr># shft-&rarr;</nobr>	slur end # notes right	.
